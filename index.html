Our Story

Founded in 2015, Goa LED Repair started with one goal: to provide fast, reliable, and honest repair services to the homes and businesses of Goa. We saw too many people throwing away perfectly good electronics. We knew we could provide a better, more affordable, and more sustainable solution.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to get your electronics working like new, with minimal hassle for you. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing, certified technicians, and a warranty on all our work. We're not just a business; we're your neighbors.
